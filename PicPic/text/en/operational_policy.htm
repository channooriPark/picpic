PicPic Operational Policy


This operational policy stipulates the standard for service provided by PicPic and the detailed item which customers shall comply with, in order to deal against the problem, with a consistent, which could occur in the service, while operating the service provided by PicPic. In the event you do not observe the operational policy, it results in the disadvantage to you, it is recommended to read it carefully. PicPic tries to establish the reasonable operational policy through continuous exchange of opinions with customers so as to improve the policy related to the service, in this case it shall be notified at least prior to 7 days through the service notice bulletin board provided by PicPic.

Right and Obligation of Member

A. Right of Customer

Customer can request and suggest including various inquiries for or related to the service provided by PicPic, through on-line, phone counseling.
[On-line Inquiry] Counseling available time : 24 hours
[Phone counseling] Monday ~ Friday (10:00~18:00), Sat / Sun / public holidays off

  1. PicPic shall not get involved or take measures for the dispute occurred between customers and the damage resulted from customer's own fault. However, in the event being damaged by violation of operational policy by other customer while using the service, customer can request application of operational policy by reporting it to PicPic, in this case PicPic checks the contents of report and can take disciplinary measure in accordance with the operational policy.
  2. In case that result of application of operational policy is dissatisfactory, objection can be applied to PicPic by the legitimate way through the homepage anytime.

B. Obligation of Customer

  1. Customer shall report PicPic for the problem such as bug or system error, etc. found during use of service, and shall not spread to other customer or abuse it. After the problem such as bug or system error, etc. occurs, if a customer use it for the purpose of obtaining the benefits without reporting to PicPic., it can be considered as abuse of bug and system or he(she) can be regarded as such user, it is the behavior to cause harm to the system itself and other customer, he(she) may be restricted in use of service.
  2. Customer can join to membership in order to use the service provided by PicPic, and the correct information should be entered at joining, in case of joining by entering the false information or by using the personal information of other person by theft, there could be a restriction in use of service and protection of rights cannot be claimed.
  • Customer shall not do the words and actions violating the related laws in the service.
  • Customer shall not ridicule a specific nationality, race, religion, ethnicity, gender, disability, etc or shall not do harm unilaterally
  • Customer shall not slander a specific nationality, race, religion, ethnicity, gender, disability, etc or shall not do a collective action for the anti-social, unlawful purpose.
  • Customer shall not report the false information in relation to other customer in the service.
  • Customer shall not do activity of advertising / public relations for the commercial purpose.
  • Customer shall not use the illegal software/hardware which could influence to the service.
  • Customer shall not give a displeasure to other customer by the sexually explicit expression.
  • Customer shall not use the common taboo word such as abuse, slang, jargon.
  • Customer shall not do action to impersonate the executive and employee of the company or the staff associated with the service, especially shall not do action giving damage or confusion to other customer such as cheating other customer or taking a benefit, etc.
  • Besides, customer shall not do abnormal things by social norms, common sense being shared between the company and customer.

Policy Related to Restriction in Use of the service

A. Posting material corresponding to the reason for restriction in use

Posts of pornography

  • In case of excessive body exposure or description of lewd behavior
  • In case of sharing sex trafficking-related information
  • In case of including information which induces the sexual shame or discomfort to other person

Malignant code spreading posts

  • In case of writing or inducing of installation, distributing the program disturbing the work of PicPic, using the program baffling for service environment of user such as malignant code, virus

Posts violating the intellectual property rights

  • In case of sharing the copyrighted material of other person such as broadcasting, sound source, movie, cartoon, etc. without consent of the party
  • In case of providing the information or method to acquire the copyrighted material of other person through illegal route (for instance : free download, free server, share of CD key, etc.)
  • The content invading the trade marks, design rights belonging to the right of other person without permission.
  • In case of using my posting material (whole or part), photograph, image by theft (illegal download)

Illegal Posts

  • In case of promoting the illegal gambling site
  • In case of promoting or selling the illegal product or good being forbidden to sell on the internet
  • In case of motivation and proving the helpful information for execution of criminal behavior
  • In case of writing or inducing of installation, distributing the program disturbing the work of PicPic, using the program baffling for service environment of user such as malignant code, virus

Others

  • In case of abnormal posts inserted repeatedly with the specific word/sentence, or filling up with the mechanical pattern words using program, etc
  • in case of filling up with the chain letters of fortune, spam articles, promotional articles, etc.
  • In case of using the service differently from the original purpose of PicPic service, such as inducting exposure of personal information of other person, impersonating of administrators, etc.

B. Restriction in use for Improper Posts

Limit of Posts

  • The exposure of improper post shall be blocked out by administrator so that other visitor cannot read it.
  • The blocked post shall be indicated as "the post blocked out by administrator " so that other visitors can recognize it.

Restriction of account

  • For the user who write the improper posting material by this operational policy repeatedly, his(her) account shall be restricted temporarily or permanently.

Though it does not match to the above item concretely, use of service can be restricted by company agreement in case that it adversely affect provision of the healthy service environment, weighted treatment can be made to the duplicated case.
